I am a research assistant at the DataSEARCH research group in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Moratuwa. I am currently working on the super resolution of microscopy images using deep learning techniques.

I received my undergraduate (BSc (Hons) in Engineering) and postgraduate (MSc by Research) degrees from the University of Moratuwa.

My research interests include studying the microstructures within 3D materials to expand their uses and improve our understanding of these materials. I also work on analysing materials using multimodal imaging and computer vision, particularly focusing on computational X-ray microanalysis and its complexities.

Sustainability and decarbonisation serve as fundamental principles guiding my research endeavours. Every facet of my research work is scrutinised through the lens of these principles. I am also keen on applying advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques to areas like microscopy and spectroscopy.
