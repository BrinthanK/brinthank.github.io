Greetings! I am currently completing my master's degree (major component of research), at the University of Moratuwa. I completed my undergraduate studies in Earth Resources Engineering at the same university, graduating with a BSc (Honours) degree.

My research during my master's program centers on characterising microstructures in 3D materials to expand their applications and deepen our understanding of these substances. My research interests lie in material characterisation through multimodal imaging and computer vision, particularly focusing on computational X-ray microanalysis and its fundamental challenges. Sustainability and decarbonisation are core principles guiding my research, and I explore every facet of my work with these in mind. Additionally, I am actively investigating advanced machine learning and deep learning methodologies for their potential applications in microscopy and spectroscopy.
