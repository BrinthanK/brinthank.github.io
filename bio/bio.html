    DataSEARCH

    Computer Science & Engineering
    University of Moratuwa
    In my current research, I am focussing on single image super-resolution (SISR) for microscopy images using cGANs. Specifically, I am working on cGANs to enhance the structural resolution of images obtained from scanning electron microscopes (SEM) and optical microscopes. My work involves assessing the capabilities of existing SISR algorithms to improve resolution without compromising the structural integrity of the images.

    Master of Science (Major Compoenent of Research)

    Earth Resoruces Engineering
    University of Moratuwa
    Period: September 2022 - April 2024

    This research has focussed on characterising the heterogeneity of coal fly ash waste, a by-product of coal combustion, using microscopy, spectroscopy, and image processing techniques. I specifically worked on classifying cenospheres (nano to micron-sized hollow spheres) among microspheres (solid spheres) using EDS. During this study, I developed a Python package, pyDeepP2SA, using the Segment Anything Model from Meta AI and computational X-ray micro-analysis. I also worked on developing a more reliable and non-invasive method to quantify the imperceptible structures of nano and micron-sized materials using the same EDS scans. I have since transitioned away from contributing to the coal fly ash and cenosphere domain.
